Palbook — Palworld Crafting & Breeding Calculator
==================================================

HOW TO USE
  1. Unzip this whole folder anywhere (Desktop is fine).
  2. Double-click  Palbook.bat
  3. Your browser opens the app. Keep the black window open while
     you use it; close it to stop Palbook.

If Windows shows a blue "Windows protected your PC" screen the first
time, click "More info" then "Run anyway" — it happens to anything
downloaded from the internet.

Nothing is installed and nothing touches the internet: the app runs
entirely on your own machine (a tiny local server on localhost:8731).

WHAT'S INSIDE
  Crafting: pick an item, rarity tier (Common -> Legendary) and
  quantity — get the full production tree and a consolidated raw
  materials shopping list. Data scraped from paldb.cc.
  Breeding: parent pair -> offspring, and reverse lookup.
  (Breeding numbers are still placeholder data for now.)
